Devtron was established in 2019 by industry veterans with more than 30 years of experience under their belt. With some of the most technologically advanced companies of their sector as customers, Devtron is redefining the paradigm of how DevOps should be done. It’s flagship platform - Release Orchestration and Application Lifecycle Management on Kubernetes - is Open source and has helped companies around the world reduce their go to market time in a secure, stable and cost effective manner. It’s built on some of the most loved technologies like GoLang, React, Kubernetes, TypeScript to name a few.

About the role:

The Developer Evangelism team supports and grows Devtron’s community by engaging with Devtron’s community members through content and conversations.As a Developer Evangelist, you will help us fulfill our mission by connecting with other developers, contributing to open source, and sharing your knowledge and experience about Devtron and other leading technologies at conferences and meetups, in contributed articles, and on blogs, podcasts, and social media. Your work will foster a community inspired by Devtron.We focus on generating awareness about Devtron by rolling up our sleeves, contributing to the ecosystem, and enabling others to become evangelists outside the company as well. Not afraid to be hands-on, you might write sample code, author client libraries and work with strategic Devtron partners and customers to excite and engage our developer communities.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Lead the conversation around the latest technology advancements and best practices around kubernetes and devops in the developer community
  • Create engaging content, including technical talks, blog posts, demos, and videos that educates developers on kubernetes and devops technologies and trends
  • Support Devtron product and engineering efforts by sharing what you learn while engaging with the wider Devtron community and the tech community, at large
  • Contribute to relevant open source projects, foundations, and TAGs in order to give Devtron visibility and share our experience and insights to developments in our areas of interest
  • Be a leader within Devtron and in the DevOps community

Must have:

  • 8 to 12 years of overall experience
  • Experience building software and contributing to open source in the cloud computing ecosystem
  • Meaningful social presence with engaged followers
  • Ability to manage the fast moving conference schedule with its CFP deadlines and show dates
  • Self-directed and work with minimal supervision
  • Outstanding written and verbal communications skills with the ability to translate complex technology concepts into simple and intuitive communications
  • Ability to travel up to 30% of the time (Depending on Covid situation)
  • 2-3 years experience giving talks and developing demos, webinars, videos, and other technical content to audiences of 100 and larger

Good to have:

  • Good exposure to cloud (AWS/GCP/Azure) and Kubernetes
  • Knows agile development best practices and has scrum exposure. Experience working with geographically distributed teams
  • Understanding of various DevOps technologies

Education:

Bachelors/Masters (preferably BE/B. Tech/M. Tech/ M.S.) - Computer Science IT
